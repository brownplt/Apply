welcome

Welcome to Apply, a graduate admissions application developed in-house that the department is trying out this fall. Its goal is to augment the current admissions process used by the department with some useful tools for reviews and communication. The application is based on Resume, the faculty recruiting system built by Arjun Guja and Jacob Baskin. The current maintainer is Joe Politz, email him at joe@cs.brown.edu with any problems, suggestions, or concerns.
